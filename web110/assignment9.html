<b> Painting </b>

Painting has been my favorite hobby for as long as I remember. I have been going to a private art studio since the age of 4. The teachers at this studio was a married couple from Poland, they started out the studio in their basement and it eventually big enough to them purchasing a location of their own for the business. They taught me many things in many different forms of art, like pottery, sculpting, wood carving, drawing, and of course painting. Oil painting has become my favorite over the years, but I do still paint in acrylic, gouache, or watercolor from time to time. Painting has become my favorite because it allows me to focus
